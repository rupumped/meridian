Meridian is a free and open-source time zone synchronizer that helps you coordinate meetings and events across different time zones.

<b>Features:</b>

• Add multiple time zones to compare times side-by-side
• Drag horizontally to explore different times of day
• Visual day/night indicators for each timezone
• Click any time slot to create calendar events
• Export events as .ics files or directly to Google Calendar
• Copy event details with times in all your tracked zones
• Customize labels for each timezone
• Reorder timezones by dragging
• Switch between 12-hour and 24-hour time formats
• Share your timezone configuration via URL
• Works offline after first visit

<b>Perfect for:</b>

• Remote teams working across continents
• Scheduling international meetings
• Coordinating with family and friends abroad
• Planning travel itineraries
• Managing global project deadlines

<b>Privacy-focused:</b>

• No account required
• No tracking or analytics
• All data stored locally in your URL
• No server-side storage
• Works completely offline
